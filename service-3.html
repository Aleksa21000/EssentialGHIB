AD&D/Life - insurance

Adding accidental death and dismemberment, which is a policy that pays to a beneficiary when death is caused by an accident, is an affordable way to round out an employee benefit plan. This benefit can be in addition to a members existing Life policy, helping when death or serious accident could keep them from providing for their families.
